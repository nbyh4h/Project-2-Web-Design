About Us

Our History

Established in 1995, Medi Health & Care has been serving the healthcare needs of the Malaysian community for over two decades.

We take pride in our journey, which began as a small neighborhood pharmacy and has since grown to become a reliable healthcare

partner for people from all walks of life. Our commitment to providing top-notch pharmaceutical services and products has earned

us the trust and loyalty of our valued customers.

Our Vission

Our Vission

At Medi Health & Care, our vision is to be the leading pharmacy in Malaysia, recognized for our unwavering dedication to the

well-being of our community. We aspire to be the first choice for healthcare solutions, embracing innovation and technology to

enhance the quality of life for our customers.

Our Mission

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accessible, affordable, and personalized healthcare services to every individual who walks through our doors.

To achieve this, we focus on:

Quality

Innovation

We continuously seek innovative solutions to better serve our customers and stay at the forefront of healthcare trends.
